This nonfiction account of one man's seven mile journey into the wilderness alternates between bleak and beautiful. Author Charlie Parker maintained the appropriate body fat while being in the wilderness for more than five hours, built one fire, and considered building a canoe out of one of the trees along the riverbank. His next work is forthcoming from "Becoming Press", a forthcoming press.
